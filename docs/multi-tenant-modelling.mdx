### 2. Create Notification Categories

Create categories mapping to your HRTech modules and survey types. Use **Sections** for modules and **Sub-categories** for survey types.

**For Acme Corp:**
- `perform.review_reminder` - Performance review reminders (Q1 2025 cycle)
- `engage.engagement_survey` - Employee engagement surveys
- `onboard.welcome_sequence` - New hire welcome emails

<Tip>
Group similar notifications together. For Acme Corp's Q1 2025 Performance Review, group all steps (invite, reminder 1, reminder 2) under `perform.review_reminder` instead of separate categories.
</Tip>

### 4. Create Templates

Create templates for each survey type and notification kind. For Acme Corp's Performance Review, create `performance-review-invite-email`. Use tenant variables for automatic branding.

**Naming:** `SURVEY_{TYPE}_{KIND}_{CHANNEL}`

**Variables:**
- **Data:** `{{review_cycle}}` = "Q1 2025", `{{due_date}}`
- **Tenant:** `{{$tenant.logo}}`, `{{$tenant.primary_color}}`, `{{$tenant.tenant_name}}` = "Acme Corp"
- **Recipient:** `{{$recipient.name}}`, `{{$recipient.email}}`

When Acme Corp launches Q1 2025 Performance Review, SuprSend automatically applies Acme Corp's branding.

## How It Works

When Acme Corp's platform triggers a Q1 2025 Performance Review invitation, SuprSend handles everything automatically:

<Steps>
  <Step title="1. Platform Identifies Tenant">
    Platform identifies `tenant_id: "acme-corp"` when triggering the workflow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="2. Apply Tenant Preferences">
    Checks Acme Corp's defaults for `perform.review_reminder`: Email enabled, SMS disabled, Slack enabled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="3. Apply Employee Preferences">
    Employee opts out of Slack â†’ Final channels: Email only.
  </Step>
  <Step title="4. Resolve Template">
    Checks for Q1 2025 variant. Falls back to `performance-review-invite-email` with Acme Corp branding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="5. Render Branding">
    Applies Acme Corp's logo, colors, and company name to template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="6. Route via Vendor">
    Routes through Acme Corp's SMTP if configured, otherwise platform default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="7. Deliver">
    Email sent with Acme Corp branding and Q1 2025 cycle details.
  </Step>
</Steps>
